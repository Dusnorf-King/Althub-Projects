My Favorite Snack Recipe

This is a simple, nutrient-dense and delicious breakfast recipe that you can make in no time! It is a popular appetizer, snack, or can even be served as a breakfast or brunch item.

The filling typically includes shredded chicken, and can be enhanced with various vegetables, seasonings, sauces, and cheeses.

Recipe Information

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 5
  • Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 10 bread slices
  • 1 cup cooked shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage, a few rings of onions and cucumbers
  • 2 whole sliced tomatoes and lettuce and some cabbage
  • Sachet mayonnaise and Mustard

Instructions

  1. Toast the Bread, lightly toast two slices of bread (or a bun/roll) for crunch and flavor.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables by washing and slice fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers and onions).

  3. Assemble the Sandwich and Spread a condiment (mayonnaise, mustard) on the bread. Layer vegetables first, then add the chicken on top.

  4. Cut the Sandwich into halves or quarters for easy handling then finish and Serve by placing the second slice of bread on top, press gently, and cut in half if desired.

  5. You can the sandwich with honey drizzles and some freshly squeezed orange juice or a cup of hot tea or coffee or even a bottle of cold fizzy drink. Whatever you decide to serve it with, just make sure you enjoy every bite!

And voila! sandwich is served.
